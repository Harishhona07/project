ABOUT US

Los Pollos Hermanos

Los Pollos Hermanos is the best fast-food restaurant chain that is specialized in fried chicken operating across the southwestern United States. It translates for "The Chicken Brothers." Founded by Gustavo Fring and Max Arciniega, the restaurant chain has fourteen locations throughout the southwest.
